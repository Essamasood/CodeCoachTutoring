FAQ

Common questions.

Do I need any experience to start?

None at all. CodeCoach is specifically designed for complete beginners. If you have never written a single line of code, you are exactly who this is built for. Your tutor will start from the very beginning — no assumptions made.

Why JavaScript specifically?

JavaScript is the most widely-used beginner language in the world. It runs in every web browser, you can see results instantly without installing anything special, and it opens the door to web development, app building, and more. It is the best first language to learn.

What do I need for a session?

A computer (Windows or Mac), a stable internet connection, and a free Zoom account. That is it. No software purchases, no downloads — your tutor will walk you through setting up a free coding environment in your browser during the first session.

How long until I can actually build something?

Most students build their first small interactive program within the first two or three sessions. By week six to eight of consistent sessions, you will have built a real mini-project you can show people. Progress depends on how often you practice between sessions too.

Can a parent book on behalf of their child?

Absolutely. Many of our students are booked by parents. The session itself is between the student and the tutor, but parents are welcome to join the intro call and ask any questions they have. Sessions are always conducted professionally over Zoom.

What if I miss a session or need to reschedule?

No problem — just cancel or reschedule at least 24 hours before your session through the booking link in your confirmation email, and there is no charge. We understand that schedules change, especially over the summer.
